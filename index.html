Spicy potatoes


This is a recipe that I have used over and over, it is easy and a guaranteed hit. I made them for my cousin last night and she raved about them. Today she had the leftovers for lunch at work and sent me a text saying (again) how delicious they were. You will not be disappointed! Potatoes are crispy on the outisde, soft and fluffy on the inside. Yummy! Here's what you will need and how to make them.


Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 lb Russet or Yukon Gold potatoes (scrubbed clean, skin on)
¼ cup Olive oil
½ teaspoon crushed red chili flakes, smoked paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, cumin, black peppper and salt

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven at 415°F
  2. Cut potatoes into 1-2inch cubes, toss in bowl with spices and olive oil until well-coated
  3. Spread in single layer on baking sheet and bake for 20-30 minutes (depending on the size of your cubes) until golden brown and crispy

This recipe can be used with sweet potatoes as well, they will come out just as delicious however they may not come out as crispy as white potatoes

Marinated Pork Tenderloin


Another favorite. I found it on the Allrecipes website. It pairs well with steamed vegetables and rice.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
¼ cup soy sauce
¼ cup packed brown sugar
2 tablespoons sherry
1½ teaspoons dried minced onion
1 teaspoon cinnamon
2 tablespoons olive oil
1 pinch garlic powder
2 * ¾ pound pork terderloins

Preparation

  1. Place soy sauce, brown sugar, sherry, dried onion, cinnamon, olive oil, and a touch of garlic powder in a large resealable plastic bag. Seal, and shake to mix. Place pork in bag with marinade, seal, and refrigerate for 6 to 12 hours.
  2. Preheat grill for high heat.
  3. Lightly oil grate. Place tenderloins on grill, and discard marinade. Cook 20 minutes, or to desired doneness. Slice into medallions, and serve.

Double-Chocolate Mousse


This is an easy recipe I found while browsing the Kraft Canada website. From prep to serve in about 35 minutes? Yes, please!

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1½ cups skim milk, divided
1 oz. Baker's Semi-Sweet Chocolate
2 pkg. (4-serving size) Jell-O Chocolate Fat Free Instant Pudding
1 cup plus 2Tbsp. thawed, Cool Whip Light Whipped Topping, divided
½ cup fresh raspberries

Preparation

  1. Microwave 1 cup milk and chocolate in large microwaveable bowl on MEDIUM 2 min.; whisk until chocolate is completely melted and mixture is well blended. Add remaining milk and dry pudding mix; beat 2 min. Refrigerate 20 min.
  2. Whisk in 1 cup Cool Whip; spoon into dessert dishes.
  3. Top with remaining Cool Whip and berries.
